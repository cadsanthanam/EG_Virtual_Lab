Failure Case Study: When Graphics Go Wrong

Result: The "Galloping Gertie" bridge twisted and collapsed in mild winds.

Lesson: A pretty drawing ‚â† a functional design. Always validate with physics!

Timeline of Iconic Designs

1500: Leonardo da Vinci

Da Vinci's helicopter sketch established technical drawing techniques still used today.

1795: Gaspard Monge

Developed the theory of descriptive geometry, the mathematical foundation of engineering drawing.

1960s: CAD Revolution

Ivan Sutherland's Sketchpad system pioneered computer-aided design at MIT.

2000s: 3D Printing

Digital design files enable direct manufacturing from 3D models.
